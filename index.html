Selected Leadership Projects

Cloud Modernization & Legacy Migration (AWS)

AWS Aurora PostgreSQL Redshift Migration Factory
  • Business challenge: Legacy on-prem databases increased cost, slowed delivery, and raised reliability/compliance risk.
  • Strategic solution: Led modular migration pattern to isolated Aurora PostgreSQL clusters; centralized OLAP in Redshift; standardized CI/CD and data quality gates.
  • Impact: Reduced licensing/storage overhead (~40%); improved performance and delivery velocity; enabled governed, reusable analytics datasets.

Observability Platform (DART) for Data Reliability

Observability Lineage SLOs SOC-ready
  • Business challenge: Data incidents (late/missing/incorrect data) degraded trust and caused downstream operational churn.
  • Strategic solution: Designed end-to-end monitoring for pipelines, freshness, quality, and lineage; automated alerting + runbooks; standardized incident response.
  • Impact: Faster detection and resolution, fewer recurring incidents, improved stakeholder confidence, and better audit readiness.

Customer Retention & Predictive Analytics (Churn / Propensity)

ML Enablement Feature Store Activation MLOps
  • Business challenge: Reactive retention strategies led to missed opportunities and inefficient outreach.
  • Strategic solution: Built scalable feature pipelines; productionized scoring and activation feeds; integrated results into business workflows and dashboards.
  • Impact: Improved targeting and intervention timing; reduced avoidable churn; supported measurable uplift tracking and experimentation.

Omni-Channel Analytics & Self-Service Reporting

BI Semantic Layer Governance Enablement
  • Business challenge: Disconnected channel data limited visibility into customer journey and performance drivers.
  • Strategic solution: Implemented curated conformed models + semantic layer; standardized metric definitions; governed access and documentation.
  • Impact: Faster decision cycles, fewer conflicting dashboards, and improved alignment across teams.

Cloud Data Lake on GCP (GCS) & Pipeline Standardization

GCP GCS BigQuery ELT/ETL
  • Business challenge: Fragmented ingestion patterns increased operational cost and slowed onboarding of new data sources.
  • Strategic solution: Standardized ingestion frameworks; implemented curated zones; improved governance, metadata, and lifecycle controls.
  • Impact: Faster data onboarding, higher reuse, and more predictable reliability/performance.
